What is WiiStream?

Why I'm glad you asked!

WiiStream is a small team of developers planning to revive and maintain streaming apps for the Wii. The image is to restore services that used to run on the Wii!

Discussion and coordination happen on Discord. If you want to contribute, chat with others or just say hi, check it out!
